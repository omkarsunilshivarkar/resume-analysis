How does Resumetrics work ?

Resumetrics is an easy to use resume analysis website with some amazing features which enhance its usability.

Company - Choose Your Preference
Once you login to your profile, select your preferences and desired openings position.
Candidate - Choose Your Preference
Once you login to your profile, select your desired company and corresponding openings/position.
Company - Select Your Dream Employee
Next, you would be presented with the list of all employees who have applied for a corresponding positon and their resume analysis.
Candidate - Upload Your Resume
Once you fill in your details you can upload your resume and then answer some questions related to the position you are applying.
